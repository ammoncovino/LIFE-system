## Revenue Stack System

**Revenue Sources:** Ticket sales, food, alcohol, parking, vendors, sponsorships, lodging (narration suites).

**Rule:** Never give away multiple revenue layers in one deal.

---

## Sponsorship Model

**Current Idea:** 50% sponsor share with promoter.

**Risk:** Sponsor ownership drift. Brand dilution.

**Control Rule:** Venue retains approval. Sponsorships align with brand.

## Court Utilization System

**Assets:** 4 pickleball courts, underutilized for 4–6 months.

**Time Segmentation:**
- Morning: schools, camps
- Afternoon: training, youth programs
- Evening: leagues
- Weekend: tournaments / events

**Users:** Pickleball leagues, corporate events, fitness groups, schools, tournaments.

**Strategy:** Multiple operators filling different time blocks.

---

## Honcho Contract Model (Evaluated)

**Key Terms:** 5 seasons (~50 weeks total). 2 courts. Sunday 3pm–8pm. $10/hour per court.

**Financial Output:**
- $10 × 2 courts = $20/hour
- $20 × 5 hours = $100 per day
- ~$1,000 per season
- ~$5,000 total

**Issues Identified:** Underpriced. Marketing burden on venue. No upside participation. Long-term lock without performance.

**Recommended Fix:** Raise to $25–40/hour OR $10/hour + 15–20% of player revenue.

## Pickleball Strategy Model

**Goal:** Fill unused court capacity.

**Approach:** Do not rely on one operator. Layer multiple users.

**Target Groups:** Local pickleball leagues, social sports companies, corporate teams, schools, tournament organizers.

**Key Rule:** Courts are activation space, not just rental space.

## Food & Retail Strategy

**Current:** Venue operates food. Food truck on-site. Alcohol controlled internally.

**Proposed Shift:** Bring in operator partner. Reduce operational burden.

**Rejected Model:** Full external brand takeover (Buc-ee's model).

**Correct Model:** Third-party concession operator inside your system. You retain brand, alcohol, pricing control. Operator handles execution, staffing, food production.

## Legal Structure (Honcho Agreement)

**Key Clauses:** 120-day termination without cause. Indemnification both directions. Liability limitation. Independent relationship. Texas jurisdiction.

**Critical Risk:** Refund obligation if terminated early.

## System Hierarchy Model

**Levels are:** System → Workstream → Artifact → Task.

- **System:** The full structure.
- **Workstream:** A category of work.
- **Artifact:** A document or tool.
- **Task:** An executable unit of work.
