## Diet Verification Protocol

**Purpose:** Ensure animals receive correct diet every time.

**Steps:**

- **Step 1 — Prepare:** Use written diet sheet. No substitutions.
- **Step 2 — Verify:** Confirm items, quantities, preparation.
- **Step 3 — Photograph:** Take clear photo before feeding. Post with animal name, date, initials.
- **Step 4 — Deliver:** Place food according to protocol.

**Rules:** No guessing. No "close enough." No skipping photos. If wrong: Stop → Correct → Re-photograph.

---

## Diet Photo Example Guide

**Photo must show:** Entire plate, all items visible, proper lighting, no obstruction.

**Position:** Top-down. No shadows. No filters.

**Caption format:** Animal – Date – Initials

**Errors not allowed:** Missing items. Blurry images. Late posting.

---

## Manager Sign-Off System

Managers must: Review all diet photos daily. Confirm accuracy. Respond "Verified – Initials."

If incorrect: Require correction. Document issue.

No silent approvals.

---

## Escalation Ladder

- **Strike 1:** Correct immediately.
- **Strike 2:** Formal warning + retraining.
- **Strike 3:** Disciplinary action.

Applies to staff and managers. Rule: Animal welfare overrides convenience.

## Claim-Based Work System

**Definition:** Work model where tasks are posted, individuals claim them, and ownership is explicit.

**Work Model:**
- Tasks are posted
- Tasks are claimed
- Work is verified

**Behavior Shift:** From improvisation → To authorization.

**Claim system:** Task is posted, then claimed, then executed, then verified.

**Failure modes:** Unclaimed tasks represent system breakdown. Unauthorized actions represent system drift.
