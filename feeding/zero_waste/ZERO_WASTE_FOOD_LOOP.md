# 10 — Zero Waste Food Loop System

---

## System Overview

**System Name:** Zero Waste Food Loop System
**System Type:** Closed-loop biological conversion system (Operational + Educational + Exhibit)
**Primary Purpose:** Convert 100% of uneaten food into usable biological outputs while creating a visible, teachable ecosystem

**Core Principle:** Nothing is discarded. All organic material is converted into life through structured stages.

---

## System Flow

**Main Loop:** Food waste → Compost → Insect biomass → Chickens → Eggs → Animal feed
**Parallel Loop:** Barley → Sprouting → Chicken feed → Egg production support
**Exclusion:** Outside aviary is excluded from collection process

---

## Command Structure

- System operates on CLAIMED responsibility, not passive tasks
- All tasks must be owned by a specific individual
- No floating responsibility is permitted
- Mandatory verbal claim: "I will take this assignment"
- Documentation (photos) required at every stage
- If uncertain: STOP → ASK → WAIT FOR INSTRUCTION

---

## 7 Stages

1. **Collection** — Capture ALL uneaten food from designated exhibits into ONE central bin
2. **Sorting** — Separate into: A) Primary Compost (fruits, veg, grains, plant), B) Controlled (meat, eggs, protein), C) Reject/Escalate (plastic, trash, unknown)
3. **Compost** — Break down organic material. Requires moisture balance, airflow, warmth
4. **Insect Conversion** — Black soldier fly larvae convert compost into protein biomass
5. **Chicken Feeding** — Insects + barley sprouts feed chickens
6. **Egg Production** — Chickens produce eggs (high-density nutrient package)
7. **Animal Feed** — Eggs returned to broader animal system

---
