## Venue Identity & Positioning

**Primary Brand:** Studio Giraffe

**Identity:** Studio Giraffe — Elevated Entertainment

**Function:** Multi-format entertainment venue integrated into a larger park ecosystem.

**Purpose:** Transform property into event venue, cultural hub, and revenue engine.

---

## Physical Layout & Specifications

**Indoor Space:**
- 80 x 60 ft (4,800 sq ft)
- 40 x 50 ft (2,000 sq ft)
- Total: ~6,800 sq ft

**Capacity:**
- Standing: 900–1,200
- Seated: 600–750
- Hybrid: 750–900

**Outdoor Space:**
- Front: ~500 capacity
- Rear (expandable): ~500 capacity

**Parking:** 4 acres (~600–700 cars)

**Access:** Separate venue entrance. Park access pathway (optional flow-through).

## Venue Positioning Model

Studio Giraffe is positioned as: Event venue, cultural hub, entertainment campus, multi-revenue environment.

Not: Rental hall or single-purpose concert space.

---

## Event Ecosystem Model

- **Layer 1:** Broadcast Events (UFC, boxing via Joe Hand)
- **Layer 2:** Live Events (concerts, comedy, EDM, cultural)
- **Layer 3:** Organic Events (vendors, fairs, community)

**Goal:** Stack multiple revenue streams without dependency on one.

**Rule:** Multiple revenue layers outperform single-source revenue.

---

## Promoter Partnership Model

**Structure:** Venue = platform. Promoters = content providers.

**Model B (Refined):**

**Phase 1 (Years 1–2):** 50% ticket revenue promoter / 50% venue. Venue keeps F&B, alcohol, parking.

**Phase 2 (Years 3–4):** 60% venue / 40% promoter.

**Year 5:** Renegotiation.

**Control Rules:** Venue retains alcohol. Venue retains final approval. No perpetual rights. No sub-leasing.

**Promoter Types:** Music promoters, comedy circuits, EDM operators, cultural event producers, festival organizers.

**Strategy:** Multiple promoters, not one anchor monopoly.

---

## Event Scheduling System

**Monthly Structure:**
- 2 music events
- 1 comedy event
- 1 themed event
- 1 vendor weekend
- 1 flex date

**Target:** 40–60 high-quality events annually.

**Rule:** Do not maximize quantity at the expense of quality.

---

## Vendor / Fair Model

**Current State:** 8–15 vendors. No rent charged. Vendors self-organize. Power provided. Organic growth.

**Purpose:** Increase traffic. Build community density. Create cultural activity.

**Future Option:** Introduce revenue share. Maintain low friction entry.

---

## Broadcast Event System (Joe Hand)

**Function:** Provide licensed sports broadcasts.

**Revenue:** License fee paid. Profit via food + alcohol.

**Use Case:** UFC nights, boxing events.

**Role:** Supplementary event layer, not core programming.
