## Rental info (printed on every totem)

- **Rate:** $300 per night
- **Type:** Themed indoor narration suite
- **Hours:** Check-in 7:00 PM · Program concludes 10:00 AM
- **Capacity:** Sized to group; ratio 1 staff per 30 guests minimum
- **Includes:**
  - Guided Black Light Aqua Tour at night
  - Light breakfast snack in the morning
  - Optional morning observation of select exhibits
  - Sleep within the suite — aquarium remains in natural nighttime state
- **Guest brings:** Sleeping bag or cot, pillow, toiletries, comfortable clothing
- **Rules:** No alcohol · No reaching into exhibits · No feeding unless directed · No flash · Stay with group
- **Booking:** [https://life.aquarium.com/lodging](https://life.aquarium.com/lodging) — contact venue front desk; owner approval required for group rates
- **What this is:** An overnight observation experience — not a slumber party, not a performance.
