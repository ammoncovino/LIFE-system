# DOMESTIC RABBIT

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Oryctolagus cuniculus*
> **Range:** Meadows, hedgerows, and grasslands
> **Trophic:** Grazer  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see hay — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Open meadow and hedgerow — grasses and broadleaf weeds dominate; herbs and twigs supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Grass hay** — timothy, orchard, meadow

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Dandelions** — leaves, stems, flowers
- **Clover** — red and white
- **Plantain & chickweed** — broadleaf weeds
- **Twigs & bark** — blackberry, apple, hazel

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO ICEBERG, NO CORN
No iceberg lettuce, no corn, peas, beans, or starchy roots. Hay must always be visible.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
