# SPIDER MONKEY

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Ateles geoffroyi*
> **Range:** Rainforest canopy — Mexico to Central America
> **Trophic:** Frugivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see fruit — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Tall rainforest canopy — ripe fruit dominates (≈90%); leaves, flowers, seeds supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Ripe rainforest fruit** — figs, ramón, tamarind

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Wild figs** — favored canopy fruit
- **Canopy leaves** — browse during fruit gaps
- **Flowers** — seasonal blooms
- **Young shoots** — tender new growth

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO VEG-DOMINANT BOWL
No vegetable-dominant bowls, no pellet-dominant bowls. Whole or chunked fruit must dominate.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
