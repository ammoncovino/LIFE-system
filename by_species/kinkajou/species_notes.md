# KINKAJOU

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Potos flavus*
> **Range:** Rainforest canopy — southern Mexico to Brazil
> **Trophic:** Frugivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see fruit — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Mid- to upper-canopy — fruit dominates (especially figs); nectar, flowers, occasional insects supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Wild figs** — primary canopy staple

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Nectar flowers** — important pollinator role
- **Mixed fruit** — mango, banana, papaya
- **Flowers** — rainforest blooms
- **Occasional insects** — minor supplement

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO PROCESSED SUGAR
No candy, no processed sugar, no dairy. Whole or chunked fruit must dominate.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
