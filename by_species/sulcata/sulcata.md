# SULCATA TORTOISE

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Centrochelys sulcata*
> **Range:** Sahel, sub-Saharan Africa
> **Trophic:** STRICT GRAZER (HAY-ONLY)

## 3-Second Check
Is the bowl 100% hay with desert browse only?

## The Rule
Hay-only species. No fruit, no pellets, no commercial feed.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **TIMOTHY / ORCHARD HAY** — Bulk of every meal

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **DANDELION GREENS** — Wild forage
- **HIBISCUS FLOWERS** — Wild forage
- **PRICKLY PEAR PAD** — Desert browse
- **PRICKLY PEAR FLOWERS** — Desert browse

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NEVER
No fruit. No vegetables outside the wild-range list. No pellets. No commercial feed. Pumpkin, melon, romaine, carrot, banana — all forbidden. Sulcata is the ONLY hay-only species in the building. Match the bowl. Don't interpret.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
