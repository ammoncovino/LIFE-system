# ARGENTINE TEGU

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Salvator merianae*
> **Range:** Savanna and forest edge — South America
> **Trophic:** Omnivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see protein — visible in the bowl?

## The Rule
Savanna and forest edge — invertebrates and small prey are primary; fruit and eggs supplement seasonally.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Insects & small prey** — primary protein

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Eggs** — ground-nest opportunism
- **Small prey** — feeder rodents, fish
- **Ripe fruit** — seasonal supplement
- **Wild berries** — seasonal forage

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO LIVE PREY BY GENERAL STAFF
No live prey handled by general staff. Protein must be clearly visible — no greens-or-fruit-only bowl.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
