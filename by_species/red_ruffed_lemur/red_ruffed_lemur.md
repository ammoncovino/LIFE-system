# RED RUFFED LEMUR

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Varecia rubra*
> **Range:** Rainforest canopy — northeast Madagascar
> **Trophic:** Frugivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see fruit — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Rainforest canopy — ripe fruit dominates (75-95%); nectar, leaves, seeds supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Ripe rainforest fruit** — figs, native fruits

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Wild figs** — favored canopy fruit
- **Nectar flowers** — Traveler's palm, fig
- **Canopy leaves** — dry-season fallback
- **Young shoots** — tender new growth

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO ORANGES
No oranges, no citrus, no romaine-heavy bowls. Whole or chunked fruit must dominate.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
