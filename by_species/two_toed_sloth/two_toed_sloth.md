# TWO-TOED SLOTH

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Choloepus didactylus*
> **Range:** Rainforest canopy — northern South America
> **Trophic:** Folivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see leaves — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Rainforest canopy — leaves dominate; tender shoots, blossoms, and occasional fruit supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Canopy leaves** — Cecropia, mixed browse

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Young shoots** — tender new growth
- **Blossoms** — rainforest flowers
- **Wild figs** — seasonal fruit
- **Mixed browse** — varied canopy leaves

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO CHOPPED SALAD
No chopped salad bowl, no fruit-dominant bowls. Whole leaves dominate.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
