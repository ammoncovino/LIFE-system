## The Rule
Riverine rainforest — plants and small aquatic prey both feature; balance shifts toward plants in adults.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Leaves & herbs** — primary plant matter

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Ripe fruit** — seasonal canopy fruit
- **Flowers** — wild blooms
- **Small crabs** — riverside prey
- **Small fish** — shallow-water prey

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO LIVE PREY BY GENERAL STAFF
No live prey handled by general staff. Three zones — leaves, fruit, protein — all visible.
