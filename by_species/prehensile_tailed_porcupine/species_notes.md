# PREHENSILE-TAILED PORCUPINE

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Coendou prehensilis*
> **Range:** Rainforest canopy — northern South America
> **Trophic:** Root + leaf herbivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see leaves AND a root in the bowl?

## The Rule
Rainforest canopy — leaves and stems dominate; a visible root is required; flowers and fruit supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Canopy leaves & stems** — primary forage

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Root (required)** — yam or sweet potato
- **Flowers & shoots** — tender new growth
- **Wild figs** — seasonal fruit
- **Guava** — occasional native fruit

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — ROOT IS REQUIRED
If there is no visible root, the bowl is wrong. No salad-only, no pellet-only.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
