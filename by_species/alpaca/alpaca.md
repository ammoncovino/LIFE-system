# ALPACA

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Vicugna pacos*
> **Range:** High Andes — Peru, Bolivia, Chile
> **Trophic:** Grazer  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see grass / hay — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
High-altitude bunchgrass pasture — short grasses and forbs; woody shrubs in dry season.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Grass hay** — timothy, orchard, meadow

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Fresh grass** — Andean bunchgrass
- **Forbs** — broadleaf herbs
- **Young shoots** — tender new growth
- **Wild flowers** — seasonal blooms

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO ALFALFA-DOMINANT BOWL
No alfalfa-only or pellet-heavy bowls. Grass hay is the base; rich legume hay is not.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
