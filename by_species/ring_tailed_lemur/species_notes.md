# RING-TAILED LEMUR

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Lemur catta*
> **Range:** Dry forest and scrub — southern Madagascar
> **Trophic:** Mixed primate  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see fruit, greens, AND chow / browse — all present?

## The Rule
Dry forest and scrub — tamarind dominates (≈50%); leaves, flowers, fruit, bark supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Tamarind** — fruit and leaves — ≈50% of wild diet

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Mixed leaves** — browse from many species
- **Ripe fruit** — seasonal canopy fruit
- **Flowers** — wild blooms
- **Bark & sap** — dry-season fallback

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO ORANGES, NO ROMAINE
No oranges, no citrus, no romaine-heavy bowls. Three zones — fruit, greens, chow — all visible.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
