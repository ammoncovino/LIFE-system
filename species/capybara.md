# CAPYBARA

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris*
> **Range:** Wetlands and savanna of South America
> **Trophic:** Grazer  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see grass / hay — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Wet grasslands and riverbanks — grasses, sedges, aquatic plants, occasional fruit and bark.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Grass hay** — timothy, orchard, Bermuda

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Fresh grass** — primary wild forage
- **Aquatic plants** — water hyacinth, water lettuce
- **Reeds & sedges** — emergent wetland plants
- **Bark & twigs** — occasional, dry season

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO STARCHY VEG
No corn, peas, beans, or potato. No carrot- or zucchini-heavy bowls. Hay or grass must always be visible.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
