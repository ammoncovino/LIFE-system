# PATAGONIAN MARA

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Dolichotis patagonum*
> **Range:** Patagonian steppe — Argentina
> **Trophic:** Grazer  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see grass / hay — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Arid steppe and shrubland — grasses and low shrubs; cactus, seeds, and seasonal flowers.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Grass hay** — timothy, orchard, meadow

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Fresh grasses** — Patagonian grasses
- **Cactus pads** — moisture in dry season
- **Wild flowers** — seasonal blooms
- **Wild forbs** — low broadleaf shrubs

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO STARCHY VEG
No corn, peas, beans, or carrot-heavy bowls. Grass or hay must always be visible.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
