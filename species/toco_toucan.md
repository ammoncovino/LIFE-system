# TOCO TOUCAN

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Ramphastos toco*
> **Range:** Cerrado and gallery forest — Brazil
> **Trophic:** Frugivore  ·  Wild-range diet  ·  LOW IRON CRITICAL

## 3-Second Check
Do I see fruit — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Gallery forest and cerrado — fleshy fruit dominates; insects and small prey supplement seasonally.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Low-iron fruit** — papaya, melon, fig

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Figs** — year-round canopy fruit
- **Guava** — fleshy native fruit
- **Flowers** — dry-season nectar
- **Insects** — caterpillars, termites

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO IRON-RICH FOODS
No fortified pellets, no red meat, no oranges, no citrus, no spinach. Low-iron fruit only.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
