# NINE-BANDED ARMADILLO

> [!info] Quick Card
> **Scientific:** *Dasypus novemcinctus*
> **Range:** Forest, scrub, and grassland — Americas
> **Trophic:** Insectivore  ·  Wild-range diet

## 3-Second Check
Do I see the insect diet — dominating the bowl?

## The Rule
Soil and leaf litter — invertebrates dominate; small amphibians and reptiles supplement.

## 90/10 Diet

### Base (90%)
- **Ants & termites** — primary wild prey

### Treats (10%, rotated)
- **Beetles & grubs** — main protein
- **Crickets & worms** — approved insect diet
- **Small prey** — amphibians, small reptiles
- **Eggs** — occasional opportunism

Plus other plants and prey native to its wild range.

## Taboo — NO PRODUCE-DOMINANT BOWL
No produce-dominant bowls, no pellet substitutes. Approved insect diet must dominate.

## How We Feed
- Match the bowl. Don't interpret.
- If the bowl is wrong, escalate: *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- Hold the line. Manager on duty makes the call.
