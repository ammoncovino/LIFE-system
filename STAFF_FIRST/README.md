## Locked rules — never change

These are the rules that override everything else. Memorize them.

- **Animals are TEACHERS, not attractions.**
- **Match the bowl. Don't interpret.**
- **What a living thing can sense becomes its reality. Environment shapes design.**
- **Sulcata = the ONLY hay-only species** in our canon. Russian, leopard, red-foot, and yellow-foot tortoises have different diets — see species cards.
- **If unsure: Pause → Ask → Escalate.**
- **Escalation phrase:** *"This looked like we should take it out. What would you like to do here?"*
- **Manager on duty has final say.** Never name a specific manager when escalating.
- **Posted fee, paid at the door.** No discounts without owner approval.
- **All field-trip encounters are INCLUDED.** No charging, no refusals, no reinterpretation.
